Propaganda

Propaganda posters were inspired from Uncle Sam military draft posters in 1917. Avoiding the use of weapons, the poster evokes a sense of calling to remind Eastern Seaboardians what they are protecting. The call to action are supported by a QR code that will lead the observer to the COIN Defence Force careers page. Social Media and web URLs are also available to guide the observer to the landing page.
